About the Event

YTH Rally 2026 is a transformative youth gathering designed to inspire, unite, and empower young people aged 13–21. This event brings together youth from different churches and communities for a weekend of worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth.

Throughout the rally, participants will engage in powerful worship sessions, hear inspiring messages from dynamic speakers, participate in interactive workshops, and build lasting friendships with fellow young believers. This is more than just an event — it's a movement where young hearts are ignited with passion for faith and purpose.

"Join us for an unforgettable experience that will challenge you to grow deeper in your faith, discover your God-given purpose, and connect with a community that will support you on your journey."

Whether this is your first time attending or you're a returning participant, YTH Rally 2026 promises to be an experience that will impact your life. Don't miss this opportunity to be part of something extraordinary.
